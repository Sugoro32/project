Consumer Rights

  • Right to Safety: Protects consumers from hazardous goods and services.
  • Right to Information: Consumers have the right to know the details of the products/services.
  • Right to Choose: Ensures availability of a variety of products and services at competitive prices.
  • Right to be Heard: Consumers have the right to voice their grievances and concerns.
  • Right to Redress: Consumers can seek redress for unfair practices or unsatisfactory services.
  • Right to Consumer Education: Ensures consumers are informed and can make sound decisions.
